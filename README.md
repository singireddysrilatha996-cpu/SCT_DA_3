# HR Employee Attrition Analysis Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview
This project is an interactive HR Analytics Dashboard created using Tableau as part of the SkillCraft Technology Internship – Task 03.

The objective of this project is to analyze employee attrition data and identify the key factors influencing employee turnover.

The dashboard allows users to filter and explore employee data based on department, age group, experience, and income to understand why employees are leaving the organization.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze employee attrition patterns
- Identify high-risk employee segments
- Understand the impact of salary, age, department, and experience
- Build an interactive dashboard for decision-making

---

## 📁 Dataset Used
HR Employee Attrition Dataset  
- Total Records: 1470 Employees  
- Fields: 35  
- Key Columns: Age, Department, Monthly Income, Years at Company, Attrition, Job Role, Gender

---

## 📌 Key KPIs
- Total Employees: 1470
- Employees Left: 237
- Attrition Rate: 16.1%

---

## 📊 Dashboard Components
- Attrition by Department
- Attrition by Age Group
- Attrition by Monthly Income
- Attrition by Years at Company
- Interactive Filters (Gender, Department, Age Group)

---

## 🔎 Key Insights
- Employees under 30 show higher attrition rates.
- Lower income groups have higher employee turnover.
- Research & Development and Sales departments show notable attrition.
- Employees with fewer years at the company are more likely to leave.

---

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Tableau (Data Visualization)
- Data Cleaning & Binning Techniques
- Calculated Fields (Attrition Rate)

---

## 📈 Conclusion
The analysis highlights salary, experience, age, and department as major factors influencing employee attrition.  
This dashboard can help HR teams make data-driven retention strategies.
